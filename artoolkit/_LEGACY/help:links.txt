There are four sorts of links in MediaWiki:

1.  **[internal links](/#Internal_links "wikilink")** to other pages in
    the wiki
2.  **[external links](/#External_links "wikilink")** to other websites
3.  **[interwiki links](/#Interwiki_links "wikilink")** to other
    websites registered to the wiki in advance
4.  **[Interlanguage links](/#Interlanguage_links "wikilink")** to other
    websites registered as other language versions of the wiki

Internal links
--------------

To add an internal link, enclose the name of the page you want to link
to in double square brackets. When you save the page, you'll see the new
link pointing to your page. If the page exists already it is displayed
in blue, it it does not, in red. Selflinks to the current page are not
transformed in URLs but displayed in bold.

The first letter of the target page is automatically capitalized, unless
otherwise set by the admins, and spaces are represented as underscores
(typing an underscore in the link will have a similar effect as typing a
space, but is not recommended, since the underscore will also be shown
in the text).

### How to avoid auto-links

By default, when you write a URL as is, it will be transformed to an
external link.

To avoid that effect, put the URL between tags as in:
\<pre\>\<nowiki\>http://mediawiki.org

</pre>
### Interlanguage links

If your wiki has other language versions, you may find “interlanguage
links” in the [sidebar](/Help:Navigation#Sidebar "wikilink"), just below
[toolbox](/Help:Navigation#Toolbox "wikilink") in the box named “.”

Interlanguage links behave similar to interwiki links, except that they
are listed in the sidebar. To create an interlanguage link from a page,
just type
`<nowiki>[[/</nowiki>''language_prefix'':pagename|</nowiki>''language prefix'':pagename]]`
wherever you like in the page; the language prefix is the prefix
specified at your wiki for the other language version (typically the
[ISO language code](/wikipedia:ISO_639 "wikilink")).

If you want to make the interlanguage link to appear in the content of
the page, you can add a colon before the language prefix, e. g.
`<nowiki>[[/:en:Sunflower|:en:Sunflower]]</nowiki>`.
