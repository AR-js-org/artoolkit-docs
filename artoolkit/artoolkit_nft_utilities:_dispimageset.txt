Purpose
-------

dispImageSet displays compressed image pyramids.

Usage
-----

    ./dispImageSet <filename>

After launching dispImageSet, the various image resolutions will be
displayed on screen (shrunk/zoomed as necessary to fit on screen). Press
spacebar to view the images, or esc when you're done.
